What is SIG?

▶

Signal Intensity Gradient (SIG) is a biomarker that functions beyond a simple factor for diagnosis.

it can help identify the cause and aid in prevention of various cerebrovascular diseases.

▶

SIG, as a surrogate marker, is highly correlated with shear stress, the tangential force of the

flowing blood on the endothelial surface of the blood vessel.
